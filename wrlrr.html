Wind Riders of the Lost River Range

Wind Riders of the Lost River Range was a team project using SQL for my databases class over the course of my Fall 2022 semester. In this mock situation, our team was given a memo detailing a fictional business and their needs for a new, updated database comapred to their current system. Our team planned and designed the database from the ground up, including entity relationions, queries, and stored procedures.

This project is still assigned to students at Idaho State, so any finished code from our project is unable to be shared.However, The original project was published by Communications of the Association for Information Systems in 2005 and is viewable by the public.
